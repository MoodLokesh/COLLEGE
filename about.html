About Us

Welcome to Mood Lokesh College of Engineering, where we provide an outstanding education, foster a strong sense of community, and prepare students for success in the workforce and beyond. Our commitment to academic excellence and personal growth ensures that each student is well-equipped for the challenges ahead.

Our History

Mood Lokesh College of Engineering was founded in 2025 with a vision to provide high-quality education to students from all walks of life. Over the years, we have grown into a prestigious institution known for our dedicated faculty and comprehensive academic programs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide students with a transformative education that equips them with the skills, knowledge, and values necessary to thrive in an ever-changing world. We are committed to promoting intellectual curiosity, creativity, and a passion for lifelong learning.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We believe in fostering an environment of honesty, transparency, and respect.
  • Excellence: We are committed to maintaining the highest standards in education and research.
  • Diversity: We celebrate diverse perspectives and encourage inclusivity in all aspects of our community.
  • Innovation: We encourage creative thinking and embrace new ideas to solve complex challenges.
